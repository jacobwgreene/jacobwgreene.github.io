Augmented Reality Criticism Applications

If you are browsing this site on an Android device, you can download beta-releases of the Trace ARCs applications by clicking on the links below. Before you download, allow your device to download from "Unkown Sources" in the "Security" option of your device settings. Once the .apk file is downloaded to your device, click on the notification icon, or locate and select the file in your "downloads" folder.

  • Super PAC Scramble

    • Super PAC Scramble is a single-player augmented reality game that educates players about monetary donations for the 2016 U.S. presidential election. Using campaign logos as trigger images, the game invites players to select one of seven presidential frontrunners and collect financial support for his or her campaign. By digitally dragging and dropping names and donations onto a candidateâ€™s logo, players learn which presidential contenders are and are not supported by super PACs as well as the difficulties of becoming a viable candidate through small, individual donations. Super Scramble was created by Jacob Greene and Melissa Bianchi.
