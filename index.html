I'm a Ph.D candidate studying writing and digital rhetoric at the University of Florida. My research explores the use of mobile augmented reality as a technology for writing and platform for public discourse. I currently serve as the Augmented Reality Criticisms Coordinator for Trace Innovation, a digtial humanities initiative in the UF English Department.
