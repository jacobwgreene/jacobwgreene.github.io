I am a doctoral student specializing in writing and media studies in the Department of English at the University of Florida. My dissertation research explores applications of augmented reality as a writing technology and medium for critical public discourse. My other research interests include circulation studies, visual rhetoric, composition pedagogy, and environmental rhetoric.
