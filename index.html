I'm a Ph.D candidate studying writing and digital rhetoric at the University of Florida. My research explores how mobile writing technologies such as augmented reality and locative media can be used to (re)write physical locations, objects, images, and texts. I currently serve as the Augmented Reality Criticisms Coordinator for Trace Innovation, a digital rhetoric initiative in the University of Florida English Department.
