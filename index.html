I am a doctoral student specializing in writing and media studies in the Department of English at the University of Florida. My dissertation research explores applications of augmented reality as a writing technology and medium for critical public discourse. My other research interests include circulation studies, visual rhetoric, and environmental rhetoric. For a recent project, I utilized Laurie Gries' iconographic tracking methodology to trace the visual circulation of Florida sinkholes within the mainstream media from 2013-2014. This research is included in the December 2015 issue of The Journal of Florida Studies under the title “Premediating Ecological Crisis: A Visual Rhetoric of Florida Sinkholes.”
